About

Trapa Security is an information security research firm founded in 2020.

Our team has extensive research experience in different information security fields,
including Windows, Mobile and IoT security.

We are also experienced CTF players and organizers, and have participated in international CTF tournaments as HITCON CTF Team, achieving second place at DEF CON 22, 25 and 27.
